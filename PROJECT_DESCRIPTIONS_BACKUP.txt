1. AALSI-BUDDY

### Aalsi-Buddy: The Hyper-Local Campus Ecosystem

**The Problem:** Campus life often involves small but urgent tasks (getting notes, canteen errands, or emergency supplies) that students are too busy or "Aalsi" (lazy) to do themselves, while others are available and willing to help for a small reward.

**The Solution:** Aalsi-Buddy is a real-time, PWA-enabled platform that connects the "Need" with the "Availability."

* **Smart Notifications:** Real-time push alerts for task requests even when the app is closed.
* **Anonymous Mode:** Privacy-first "Ghost Mode" for secure campus navigation.
* **PWA Integration:** Full smartphone installation support for a native app experience.

2. SILENT CLASSROOM QUERY SYSTEM (SCQS)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
### The Problem: Disruption of Instructional Flow
In most classrooms, when a student has a question or needs to step out, they usually have to interrupt the teacher mid-sentence. This breaks the focus of the entire class.

### The Solution:
SCQS allows students to send "Silent Signals" (Questions, Bathroom breaks, Help requests) directly to a dashboard on the teacher's screen.

### Key Features:
* **Real-time Queue:** Teachers see a list of student needs without a single word being spoken.
* **Priority Sorting:** Questions are ranked so the teacher can address them at the right time.
* **Status Tracking:** Log of common classroom interruptions to optimize teaching time.

3. SMART RING NFC DOOR ACCESS SYSTEM
------------------------------------------------------------------------
### The Problem: The Physical Key Hassle
In hostels and shared flat environments, managing physical keys or bulky keycards is a constant headache. Keys get lost, and carrying them during sports or late-night study sessions is inconvenient.

### The Solution:
A wearable NFC Smart Ring that integrates with a secure backend to grant door access with a simple tap of the finger.

### Key Features:
* **Secure Auth:** Encrypted NFC protocols verified by a central database.
* **Access Logs:** Real-time monitoring of entries for security.
* **Emergency Override:** Admin can grant or revoke access instantly via the WhizzyX dashboard.
